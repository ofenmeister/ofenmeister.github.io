The G.O.A.T.

written by Tim Jones

Golf has definitely seen a lot of great players gracing the game namely Jack Nicklaus, Ben Hogan, Sam Snead, Gary Player, Bobby Jones, but one player who stands apart from the others who have played the sport is none other than Tiger Woods. The now 41-year-old golf player and former World No. 1 is often considered by critics to be the most successful golfers of all time and has also been the highest-paid athletes in the world for several years.
